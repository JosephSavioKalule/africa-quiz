An Android quiz game that tests your knowledge of African capital cities, featuring an interactive 3D globe.

Pick from 54 countries, watch the globe spin to each capital, and see how well you really know the continent.

Features:
* 10 questions per round, randomly selected from all 54 African countries
* 4 choices per question with smart distractors
* Interactive 3D globe animates to each capital with a marker
* Optional 15-second countdown timer
* Green/red feedback with auto-advance
* Score breakdown with full answer review
* Fully offline — no internet connection needed
